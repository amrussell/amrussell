Project Proposal

I am thinking of creating an application for the purpose of archiving and comparing cars. The user may be able to add cars to his/her "garage," and compare their cars with others.

Main User

A dude who has multiple cars and wants to archive and share his garage. Users can compare garages.

Goals

  • Users can upload entries and pictures corresponding to their real-life garage
  • Users can compare their garages to others'
  • Users can up- or downvote others' cars
